ZotSpot -in progress-

Overview

ZotSpot is designed to be an app for discovering local activities, events, and transportation options. Whether you’re looking to bond with your friends over social activities or meet new people at community events, ZotSpot has you covered.

Problem Statement

Individuals may struggle to find accessible, enjoyable and affordable activities to do in their local areas, so we are developing a mobile application which can be used to more easily discover what they can do in the area.

Research

To further understand the challenges faced in our problem statement, my team conducted a user research survey where we asked 23 users to reflect on their experience with finding activities to do with their friends. In addition, we also conducted a competitive analysis of similar applications already on the market and took note of features that they had and lacked.

User Research Survey

Our survey suggests that there are many various reasons why it’s challenging for one to search for activities to do. Several respondents found that distance, price, finding fun activities, transportation and money were the main issues.

Stickify

Stickyfi is a sticky note board application that uses machine learning to help users categorize tasks according to specific search criteria. The goal of this app is to support users with non-linear thinking patterns to be more productive by shouldering the workload of task management. My project team and I created a Figma prototype of this application.
